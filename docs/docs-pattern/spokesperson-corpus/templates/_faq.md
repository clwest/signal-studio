The questions the spokesperson will actually field. Each answer is the **exact** response
the spokesperson should give — not a paraphrase. Keep each under three sentences.

## How much does it cost?

<Answer pointing to specific Quick facts in product chunks, not pricing inline.>

## How do I get support?

## Off-limits

- Do not answer pricing questions with numbers not in product chunks.
- Do not improvise refund or cancellation terms.
- Do not promise timelines for unshipped features.
- If asked a question that isn't covered above, the answer is: "Let me have the
  proprietor reach out — that's a question worth a real answer, not an improvised one."
